Tropical Beaches

Powder-soft sand, turquoise water and palm trees swaying in the breeze.

Our tropical beach escapes are perfect if you love warm seas, sunsets, and relaxing days by the ocean. Below are some of our most popular beach destinations around the world.

Maldives – Overwater Paradise

Famous for its crystal-clear lagoons and overwater villas, the Maldives is the definition of a dream beach escape, perfect for honeymooners and couples.

  • Best time to visit: November to April
  • Highlights: Snorkeling, scuba diving, sunset cruises, private villas
  • Ideal for: Honeymoons, anniversaries, luxury relaxation

Bali, Indonesia – Island of the Gods

Bali combines beautiful beaches with a rich culture, rice terraces and temples, making it a great choice for both relaxing and exploring.

  • Best time to visit: April to October (dry season)
  • Highlights: Kuta & Seminyak beaches, Uluwatu Temple, surf spots, beach clubs
  • Ideal for: Friends, couples, digital nomads

Phuket, Thailand – Lively Beach Escape

Phuket offers a mix of lively beaches, nightlife, island-hopping and quieter bays for relaxation.

  • Best time to visit: December to March
  • Highlights: Patong, Kata & Karon beaches, island tours, street markets
  • Ideal for: Groups of friends, younger travelers, beach & party lovers

South Coast, Sri Lanka – Golden Sands

The south coast of Sri Lanka is known for its long golden beaches, surfing spots and relaxed coastal towns like Mirissa, Unawatuna and Hikkaduwa.

  • Best time to visit: December to April
  • Highlights: Whale watching (Mirissa), surfing, beach cafés, turtle watching
  • Ideal for: Couples, families, backpackers
